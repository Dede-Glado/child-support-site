Questions

Frequently asked

Is CaseReady a law firm?
No. CaseReady is not a law firm and does not provide legal advice. We are a case preparation and attorney-matching platform built by Legally Her SEO. We help you organize your situation and connect with a licensed family law attorney in your state.
Will the other parent see what I submit?
Absolutely not. Everything you submit is completely private. We will never share your information publicly, with the other parent, or with anyone outside of the attorney you choose to work with.
Which U.S. states do you cover?
We are building our attorney partner network across all 50 U.S. states. If we don't yet have a partner in your state, we'll let you know and help point you to the right resources.
How long does everything take?
The intake form takes 10–15 minutes. The evidence checklist is ongoing — you tick items off as you gather documents. The calculator gives you an estimate in under a minute. You can do all three in one sitting or come back to each one separately.
What happens after I complete the intake?
Our team reviews your submission and prepares a structured case summary. We then connect you with a qualified family law attorney in your state who has already been briefed on your situation — so your consultation starts from a position of strength.
How is CaseReady connected to Legally Her SEO?
CaseReady is built by Legally Her SEO — a legal SEO and content agency specializing in family law. Legally Her SEO helps law firms attract and convert quality clients. CaseReady is the tool that connects those firms with real, prepared clients — you.
